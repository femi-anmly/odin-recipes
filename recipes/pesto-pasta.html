Pesto Pasta


Description


Ingredients
  • Pesto
  • Pasta
  • Parmesan

Steps
  1. Boil pasta for its recommended time.
  2. Keep 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  3. Transfer pasta to bowl and add pesto and enough pasta water to create a silky texture.
  4. Season pasta and add parmesan for desired taste.
  5. Enjoy!
