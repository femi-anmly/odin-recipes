Scrambled Eggs


Description


Ingredients
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps
  1. Beat eggs in a bowl until there are no translucent spots or streaks.
  2. Add milk and whisk again until there are no translucent spots or streaks.
  3. Gently heat pan and add butter/olive oil and then add egg mixture.
  4. Stir eggs every few seconds until it reaches the desired consistency.
  5. Remove from heat and enjoy!
