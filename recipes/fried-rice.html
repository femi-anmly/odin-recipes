Fried Rice


Description


Ingredients
  • Basmati Rice
  • Beef Stock
  • Shrimp
  • Maggi Cubes
  • Spring Onion
  • Carrots
  • Thyme
  • Salt

Steps
  1. Boil rice and add to a medium pot filled with beef stock.
  2. Cook on medium heat until the water is dried up.
  3. Fry shrimp to personal liking and cook vegetables with maggi cubes and seasonings.
  4. Combine rice, shrimp, and vegetables, and continue frying for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and enjoy!
